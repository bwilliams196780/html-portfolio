About Me

Hi, I'm Brian. I'm a 25-year-old insurance agent from Texas, redirecting my career to my true passion: software development. My goal is to build the kinds of video games I've always loved to play.

I grew up in the hood, and for a long time, my world didn't extend much past my block. I knew I wanted more for myself than what comes with being a hood rat, a feeling that often made me an outsider or an 'oreo' to my peers. That drive for more is what fuels my journey into the tech world today.

My Core Principles

My approach to work and life is shaped by a few key ideas:

  • Focus on What Matters: Inspired by The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck, I concentrate my energy on impactful goals.
  • Build for the Long Term: From A Simple Path to Wealth, I learned the value of discipline and planning for the future.
  • Embrace the Grind: My love for progression fantasies like He Who Fights with Monsters reflects my own drive for continuous self-improvement.
